Best Chocolate Chip Cookies

Provided by allrecipes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter,softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons hot water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts

Directions

  1. Beat the butter and sugars, then beat the eggs and vanilla.
  2. Dissolve the baking soda in hot water and add to mixture.
  3. stir in the flour, chocolate chips, and walnuts.
  4. Drop the dough onton a prepared baking sheet.
  5. Bake until the edges are golden brown.

This is my favorite recipe because the cookies always turn out soft and delicious. The recipe is easy to follow and uses ingredients that I always have at home.
